  • Bayeux Tapestry scene-by-scene

    • The Bayeux Tapestry is an embroidery that is in Normandy, France. It is 225.7 ft. long and 19.7 in. wide, and weighs about 771.6 lbs. It shows the events that led to the Battle of Hastings, and by extension the Norman Conquest of 1066 A.D.
